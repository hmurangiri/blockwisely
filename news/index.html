Kenya's Web3 Ecosystem: Latest News and Trends: May 24th 2023
2023-05-24

The Blockchain Association of Kenya (BAK) is seeking feedback from stakeholders in the crypto and blockchain industry regarding the Finance Bill 2023 and its impact on the taxation of digital assets. Stakeholders can provide their responses through a Google Form. The BAK will compile the feedback into a report to be submitted to the Departmental Committee on Finance and National Planning by May 20, 2023. The Finance Bill 2023 proposes a 3% Digital Service Tax on income derived from the transfer or exchange of digital assets.

  1. Bitcoin Pizza Day May 22nd:
    Bitcoin Pizza Day is just around the corner, celebrated on May 22 every year, to mark the anniversary of the first documented purchase of goods using Bitcoin. On this day in 2010, a programmer named Laszlo Hanyecz famously bought two pizzas for 10,000 BTC, which is now worth millions of dollars. Bitcoin Pizza Day has since become a symbolic event that represents the growth of the cryptocurrency industry and its increasing mainstream adoption.
