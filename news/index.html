Upcoming Events

  1. Bitcoin Pizza Day May 22nd:
    Bitcoin Pizza Day is just around the corner, celebrated on May 22 every year, to mark the anniversary of the first documented purchase of goods using Bitcoin. On this day in 2010, a programmer named Laszlo Hanyecz famously bought two pizzas for 10,000 BTC, which is now worth millions of dollars. Bitcoin Pizza Day has since become a symbolic event that represents the growth of the cryptocurrency industry and its increasing mainstream adoption.
